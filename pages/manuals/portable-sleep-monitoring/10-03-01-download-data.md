## 10.3 Data Acquisition

### 10.3.1 Downloading Data

To download data into **Somnologica Studio 5** for Embletta:

1. Open **Somnologica Studio** 5 for Embletta.
2. Disconnect the proxy from the Embletta.
3. Connect the Embletta to the computer with the USB cable or the serial download cable.
4. Click the Download Data button on the Operations sheet.
5. A progress bar will indicate the progress of the download.

Download is complete when the following message is displayed: _The download operation from My Embletta has completed successfully._
